About ReLoop

ReLoop creates a bridge between businesses with unused materials and people who can give those materials a second life.

~ Closing the loop on material waste ~

The Problem

Every year, tons of usable materials are wasted by businesses. At the same time, small businesses struggle to afford resources.

Our Solution

ReLoop connects both sides by transforming surplus materials into opportunities and creating a circular economy.

Our Mission

Our goal is to build a world where every material gets a second life instead of becoming waste.
